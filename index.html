Email Spam

Unwanted or malicious emails often containing advertisements, phishing links, or fake offers.

SMS Spam

Promotional or fraudulent text messages designed to trick users or spread malicious links.

Link / URL Spam

Suspicious or shortened links that redirect users to phishing, scam, or malware websites.

News & Content Spam

Misleading articles or promotional content created to manipulate, advertise, or deceive readers.

Phone Number Spam

Calls or messages from unknown numbers commonly used for scams, robocalls, or fraud attempts.

Social Media Spam

Fake comments, messages, or posts used to promote scams, fake profiles, or malicious content.

Website / Comment Spam

Automated or low-quality comments posted on websites to advertise products or insert harmful links.

File & Attachment Spam

Emails or messages containing malicious or deceptive attachments intended to spread malware.
